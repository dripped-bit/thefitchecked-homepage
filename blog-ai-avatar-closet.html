The Prompting Formula for Perfect Results

Structure Your Prompts Like This:

[CLOTHING TYPE] + [COLOR] + [FABRIC/TEXTURE] + [FIT/SILHOUETTE] + [DETAILS] + [STYLING] + [VIBE]

Examples:

❌ Bad Prompt: "Pretty dress"

✅ Good Prompt: "Midi dress in blush pink satin, fitted bodice with V-neckline, flowing A-line skirt, delicate lace trim, paired with nude heels, romantic and elegant"

❌ Bad Prompt: "Cool jacket"

✅ Good Prompt: "Oversized denim jacket in light wash with distressed details, boyfriend fit, rolled sleeves, vintage patches, layered over white tee, edgy casual"

❌ Bad Prompt: "Work outfit"

✅ Good Prompt: "Tailored blazer in navy pinstripe, nipped waist, peak lapels, paired with matching straight-leg trousers, white button-down, pointed-toe pumps, powerful executive"

Pro Tips:

  • Be SPECIFIC about colors (not "blue"—"navy" or "sky blue")
  • Mention fabric when it matters ("silk" vs "cotton" vs "velvet")
  • Include fit ("oversized" vs "fitted" vs "relaxed")
  • Add styling context ("tucked in" vs "layered over")
  • End with the vibe you want ("professional" vs "casual" vs "dramatic")
