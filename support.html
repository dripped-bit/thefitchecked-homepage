Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload items to my digital closet?

Navigate to the Closet tab and tap the "+" icon. You can take a photo or upload one from your gallery. Our AI will automatically categorize the item for you.

How does the Avatar Try-On work?

Go to the Home tab to view your avatar. You can dress your avatar using items from your own closet or use the "Shop & Design" feature to virtually try on new products before buying.

How do I plan a trip?

Go to the StyleHub and select Trip Planner. Enter your destination and dates (e.g., Hawaii). The app will analyze the weather and suggest a packing list and outfits.

How do I delete an item from my closet?

Tap on the item you wish to remove, then tap the trash icon or select "Remove from Closet."

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email address.

Account Data & Privacy

How do I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data (including your digital closet and avatar data), please log in to the app, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Alternatively, you can contact us below to request manual deletion.
