Privacy Policy

At TheFitChecked ("we," "us," "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • User Content (The "Virtual Closet"): Photos of clothing you upload. These images are analyzed by AI to categorize items and are stored in our database linked to your user ID.
  • Facial Data (For Avatars): If you use the "Custom Avatar" feature, you may upload a photo of your face. We use this strictly to generate a stylized 2D/3D avatar. We do not share your facial data with third parties for marketing or identification purposes.
  • Measurements & Style Preferences: Height, body type, and style preferences (e.g., "Boho," "Minimalist") used to personalize recommendations.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and time spent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide the core functionality of the App:

  • To generate AI-powered outfit recommendations based on your closet and local weather.
  • To create your personalized virtual avatar for the "Try-On" feature.
  • To suggest shopping items that match your style and budget.
  • To facilitate affiliate links and shopping transactions.

3. Third-Party Services (AI & Infrastructure)

We utilize trusted third-party services to power our App. Data sent to these services is anonymized where possible:

  • OpenAI & Claude (Anthropic): For processing style queries and outfit generation.
  • Google Firebase: For secure data storage, authentication, and hosting.
  • SERP API / Google Shopping: To fetch real-time pricing and product availability.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the App settings under Account > Delete Account, or by emailing us at dripped@thefitchecked.com.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS) protocols.
  • Encryption at Rest: Your personal data, including photos and account information, is stored in encrypted databases.
  • Secure Authentication: We use Supabase/Firebase Authentication with secure token-based sessions. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and access is limited to what is necessary for app functionality and support.
  • Third-Party Security: Our infrastructure partners (Supabase, Firebase, Vercel) maintain SOC 2 compliance and follow industry security best practices.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary to provide our services and do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Regular Security Reviews: We periodically review our security practices and update them as needed to address emerging threats.

For questions about our security practices, contact us at dripped@thefitchecked.com.
