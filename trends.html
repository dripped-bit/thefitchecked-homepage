The 10 Trends You Need to Know

Straight from Milan, Paris, and New York runways — these are the looks taking over 2026. Complete with styling tips from top fashion experts.

01
Runway Favorite

Asymmetrical Hemlines

Say goodbye to symmetry. This season's hems are artfully askew and beautifully off-balance. The handkerchief hem — once an early 2000s staple — takes on a refined elegance with floor-grazing, off-kilter silhouettes that catch the wind.

How to Style It

  • Pair with a fitted top to balance the drama below
  • Keep accessories minimal — let the hem be the star
  • Choose solid colors or subtle prints
  • Works best with strappy heels or pointed boots
Best for: Date nights, brunches, creative offices
02
Elegant & Elongating

Drop Waist & Slinky Silhouettes

Waistlines are dropping in 2026, specifically in loose, slinky silhouettes that elongate your frame. Think 1920s glamour meets modern minimalism — fluid fabrics that skim rather than cling.

How to Style It

  • Embrace the column effect — avoid belting at the natural waist
  • Opt for satin, silk, or crepe fabrics
  • Add statement earrings for a touch of sparkle
  • Perfect with a sleek updo to show off necklines
Best for: Galas, weddings, evening events
03
Romantic Revival

Lace Everything

Lace has been quietly weaving its way back, and in 2026, it makes its official statement. Lace maxi skirts, satin skirts with lace trims, flowy lace pants, and lace stockings are set to be standout pieces.

How to Style It

  • Layer lace over solid basics for depth
  • Mix textures — pair lace with leather or denim
  • Choose cream or white for a fresh, modern look
  • Add edge with zebra print shoes
Best for: Garden parties, romantic dates, summer events
04
Wild Card

Zebra & Animal Prints

Zebra stripes are stealing the spotlight with projected growth of +21% for Spring 2026. Dresses, blazers, and coordinated sets in bold animal prints are making waves across all age groups.

How to Style It

  • Go full print for maximum impact or pick one statement piece
  • Ground bold prints with black accessories
  • Pair with statement earrings for evening
  • Add sheer black tights for a polished finish
Best for: Cocktail parties, fashion events, night out
05
Color of the Season

Vanilla & Popcorn Yellow

Vanilla and Popcorn Yellow are rising fast — the perfect balance between a hue that's not too much and not too little. Estimated to increase +22% among women in Europe during Spring 2026.

How to Style It

  • Wear head-to-toe yellow for a bold statement
  • Mix with white or cream for a softer approach
  • Add gold jewelry to enhance the warm tones
  • Contrast with black or navy for a modern edge
Best for: Daytime events, vacations, spring weddings
06
Drama Queen

Statement Skirts

Explosions of tulle and bubbles of silk yield skirts that shout it out loud. A piece that can shine on its own or add an exclamation point peeking from under a ladylike frock. Don't skirt the issue!

How to Style It

  • Pair with a fitted corset top for balance
  • Add pearls for classic femininity
  • Keep makeup soft and romantic
  • Choose nude heels to elongate legs
Best for: Special occasions, photo shoots, celebrations
07
Cool & Collected

Ice Blue

Ice blue has been everywhere lately, and in 2026, it's cementing its place as a true wardrobe staple. After seasons dominated by the same familiar neutrals, this cool, refreshing hue feels like a breath of fresh air.

How to Style It

  • Style as a monochrome look for modern elegance
  • Mix with white for a crisp, clean aesthetic
  • Add silver accessories to enhance the cool tones
  • Perfect for both day and evening occasions
Best for: Office wear, lunches, transitional weather
08
Back to Basics

Classic Denim Comeback

Denim has taken a few detours lately, but for Spring/Summer 2026, this favorite material returns to form. A more fitted denim jacket is gaining popularity, especially combined with flared bootcut jeans.

How to Style It

  • Choose dark wash for a polished, elevated look
  • Pair with knee-high boots for edge
  • Add delicate jewelry for feminine contrast
  • Layer with a structured bag in neutral tones
Best for: Casual outings, weekend brunches, travel
09
Utility Chic

Denim Sets & Boiler Suits

A bevy of boilersuits in shades of workwear blue stormed the runways. Quirky styling — a turtleneck here, long silk gloves there — elevate this utilitarian coverall from function to fashion.

How to Style It

  • Try a vest and wide-leg combo for a modern take
  • Add white pointed heels for instant elevation
  • Accessorize with gold buttons and details
  • Style with oversized sunglasses for off-duty cool
Best for: Creative workplaces, gallery openings, city exploring
10
Vintage Glamour

Brooches & Vintage Accessories

Brooches are proving to be an easy way to add personality to any look — or revive a piece you already own. This trend leans into vintage pins, crystal clip-ons, and heirloom-style details.

How to Style It

  • Pin on lapels, knits, scarves, or even bags
  • Cluster multiple brooches for a collected look
  • Mix vintage cameos with modern outfits
  • Add to a simple blazer for instant polish
Best for: Any occasion — the ultimate versatile trend
