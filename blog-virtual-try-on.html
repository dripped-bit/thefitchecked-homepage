Enter Virtual Try-On: The Technology That Changed Everything

Virtual Try-On (VTO) technology uses augmented reality (AR) and artificial intelligence to let you see clothes, makeup, accessories, and more on your actual body before you buy.

Not on a random model. Not on a mannequin. On YOU.

Think of it like a magic mirror in your phone that shows you exactly how that $200 jacket will look on your shoulders, how those jeans will hug your hips, or how that lipstick shade will complement your skin tone.

How It Actually Works:

Step 1: Open your phone camera or upload a photo

Step 2: The AI analyzes your:

  • Body measurements (accurate to millimeters)
  • Skin tone
  • Body shape
  • Proportions
  • Even your lighting environment

Step 3: Overlay the clothing/product in 3D

  • Simulates fabric drape and movement
  • Adjusts for fit and sizing
  • Shows realistic textures and colors
  • Displays from multiple angles

Step 4: Make a confident purchase decision
