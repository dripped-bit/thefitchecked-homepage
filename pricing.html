Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Premium before paying?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What happens after the launch special ends?

If you sign up during the launch period (first 50 users), you'll keep the 50% discount FOREVER as long as you stay subscribed. Your price will never increase!

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! Cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students get an additional 20% off on top of any current promotions. Verify with a .edu email address.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use Stripe for payment processing - the same secure platform used by Amazon, Google, and Shopify. We never store your credit card information.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! Switch plans anytime. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be charged a prorated amount. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

What's the difference between monthly and annual billing?

Annual plans save you about 17% compared to paying monthly. Plus, you won't have to worry about price changes for a full year!
